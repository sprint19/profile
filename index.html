Questions, answered

What does Sprint19 do?
Sprint19 is a software and automation partner. We learn how your business runs, then build what fits: web and mobile apps, custom software, and Odoo automation. We have shipped products for founders and operators on four continents since 2008.
How quickly can you build my product?
Most Minimum Lovable Products launch in 10 to 12 weeks. We start with a short discovery, design and prototype for your approval, then build in weekly sprints with a demo every Friday.
Do you only work with startups?
No. We work with both startups and established SMEs. Sometimes the right answer is a custom app, sometimes Odoo, sometimes both, and sometimes a process change with no new software at all.
How do you decide between Odoo, a custom app, or both?
We diagnose before we build. We map how your business actually runs, find where time and money leak, and recommend the simplest path that fits your goals and budget.
Where are you based and who do you work with?
Our home base is Bonifacio Global City in Manila, and we partner with clients across the US, Australia, Europe, and Southeast Asia. Our longest partnership has run for more than a decade.
How do we get started?
Book a free strategy call. We will review your goals, surface the highest-impact opportunities, and give you honest next steps. No pitch, no pressure.
