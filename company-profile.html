Who we are

Understand first, build after

Sprint19 is a software and automation partner for founders and operators. We diagnose before we build: we map how a business actually runs, find where time and money leak, and recommend the simplest path that fits. Sometimes that is a custom app, sometimes Odoo, sometimes both, and sometimes a process change with no new software at all.

Our mission

Help businesses run better. We learn how the work really happens before we build anything, then build only what fits.

Our vision

Software that fits the business. No company should bend itself to fit its tools; we build technology around how the business already works.

What drives us

  • Be professional. Say what needs saying, with respect and zero ego.
  • Own it, solve it, ship it. Face challenges head-on.
  • Create value through outcomes that matter.
  • People over process. Trust and connection come first.
  • Get better every day. Continuous improvement is the rhythm.
  • Have fun. We make space to enjoy the work and the people.
